MCA Course Structure


 
 
The M.C.A. Programme is a Full-timeTwo years Master's Degree Course of Computer Applications affilaited to Savitribai Phule Pune University(SPPU) and Approved by AICTE New Delhi.
 
The course imparts the knowledge and skills required for planning, designing and development of  Complex Software Applications . 

The basic objective of the Master of Computer Application (MCA) is to provide a steady stream of necessary knowledge, skills and foundation for acquiring a wide range of rewarding careers into rapidly expanding world of Information Technology

 
Eligibility for Admission
The eligibility criteria for admission for the MCA course will be as decided by the All Indian Council of Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi and Directorate of Technical Education (DTE), Government of Maharashtra. It will published on their respective websites time to time.
Any Graduate (50% Marks for OPEN Category, 45% marks for RESERVED categories) with valid MAH-MCA-CET Score. Mathematics subject is  compulsory at 10th / 12th / Graduation Level.
 
Selection Basis
The selection would be done as per the guidelines given by the Director of Technical Education, Maharashtra State, time to time.
 
 
Job Opportunities after MCA
The wide range of career options after MCA include specialized profiles in software development, hardware technology, systems development,software testing, management information system, internet, and networking.
